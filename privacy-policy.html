Privacy Policy for Raynet Bridge App

Last updated:

This privacy policy describes how [YOUR_COMPANY_NAME] (“we”, “us”) processes data received via Facebook Lead Ads to create leads in Raynet CRM.

What data we process

  • Lead form fields submitted on Facebook (e.g., name, email, phone, and any custom fields configured in the form).
  • Lead metadata (e.g., lead ID, form ID, ad ID, created time) required for troubleshooting and audit logs.

Purpose of processing

Data is used solely to insert leads into the Raynet CRM account designated by the Facebook Page owner and to provide basic diagnostics (success/failure logs).

How we process and store data

  • We do not sell or share lead data with third parties.
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS from Facebook to our server and forwarded to Raynet CRM via their API.
  • We do not permanently store lead content on our servers; minimal logs (timestamps, IDs, status) may be retained for up to 30 days for support and fraud prevention.

Legal basis

Processing is based on the legitimate interests of the Page owner to handle lead inquiries and on user consent provided when submitting the Facebook lead form.

Data deletion

User Data Deletion

If you submitted a Facebook Lead Ads form and want us to delete your data processed by the Raynet Bridge App, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to vajuucs@gmail.com with subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include your full name, email/phone used in the form, and—if available—the date and Page/form name.
  3. We will delete logs we control and forward a deletion request to the corresponding Raynet CRM account, if applicable.

We aim to complete requests within 30 days.
